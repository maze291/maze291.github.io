Method

Directional career intelligence, not a job board.

What it measures

Reviewed market records are cleaned, deduplicated where possible, categorized, and converted into aggregate weekly views.

What it avoids

The public page shows summary-level outputs and examples rather than raw records.

How to read it

Counts represent reviewed roles in this dataset, not a complete count of every open job in the Gulf. Patterns are strongest when they repeat across market reads.
